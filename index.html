About Heavy Water Lodge

Nestled in the heart of Rjukan, Heavy Water Lodge offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and traditional Scandinavian charm. Ideal for adventurers, families, and groups, the lodge is designed to be your ultimate base for exploring Norway's stunning nature.

Accommodation & Layout

The lodge accommodates up to 10 guests in a 100 m² space. It features three spacious bedrooms: two rooms with four-person bunk beds and one two-person room. The cozy living room provides breathtaking mountain views, complemented by central heating and a wood-burning fireplace for a warm atmosphere.

Kitchen & Dining

The fully equipped modern kitchen includes a fridge, oven, induction cooktop, microwave, coffee maker, and toaster. Essential cookware, dishes, and utensils are provided. Guests can enjoy meals around a large wooden dining table.

Bathroom & Wellness

The lodge features two fully equipped bathrooms with showers, a private sauna for relaxation, and a dedicated drying room for wet ski and climbing gear.

Outdoor & Activities

With direct access to hiking trails, skiing, and famous ice climbing routes, the lodge is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. The terrace offers stunning sunrise and sunset views, and a BBQ area with a fire pit provides a cozy space for outdoor gatherings.

Parking & Accessibility

The property offers free private parking and convenient access to public transport. A shuttle service is available upon request for an extra fee.

About Adventure Base

Adventure Base is designed for explorers and thrill-seekers who want to experience the best of Rjukan's outdoor activities. Whether you're a climber, hiker, or winter sports enthusiast, this lodge is your perfect retreat.

Accommodation & Layout

The lodge comfortably accommodates up to 10 guests in a 100 m² space. It features three comfortable bedrooms: two with four-person bunk beds and one two-person room. The open-plan living area is bright and spacious, offering beautiful views and a wood-burning fireplace.

Kitchen & Dining

The fully stocked kitchen includes a fridge, oven, stove, microwave, coffee maker, and dishwasher. A dining table seats up to 10 people, and meals can also be enjoyed outdoors on the private terrace.

Bathroom & Wellness

The lodge features two modern bathrooms with showers, a private sauna for relaxation, and an optional hot tub available upon request.

Outdoor & Activities

Located minutes away from world-famous climbing and ski routes, the lodge offers direct access to hiking trails and nature walks. Outdoor amenities include a BBQ area and an indoor wood-burning fireplace for cozy evenings.

Parking & Accessibility

Guests benefit from free private parking and easy access to public transport. Airport transfers are available upon request.
