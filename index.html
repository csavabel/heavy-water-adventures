About Heavy Water Lodge

Nestled in the heart of Rjukan, Heavy Water Lodge offers a perfect blend of modern comfort and traditional Scandinavian charm. Ideal for adventurers, families, and groups, the lodge is designed to be your ultimate base for exploring Norway's stunning nature.

🏡 Accommodation & Layout

- **Capacity:** Up to **10 guests**
- **Total Size:** 100 m²
- **Bedrooms:** 3 spacious rooms (2x 4-person bunk beds, 1x 2-person room)
- **Living Room:** Cozy and spacious with breathtaking mountain views
- **Heating:** Central heating and wood-burning fireplace for a warm atmosphere

🍽️ Kitchen & Dining

- Fully equipped **modern kitchen**
- **Appliances:** Fridge, oven, induction cooktop, microwave, coffee maker, toaster
- **Essentials:** Pots, pans, plates, glasses, and utensils
- **Dining Area:** Large wooden table for shared meals

🛁 Bathroom & Wellness

- **Bathrooms:** 2 fully equipped bathrooms with showers
- **Private Sauna:** Enjoy a relaxing sauna after a day of adventure
- **Drying Room:** Perfect for drying wet ski gear and climbing equipment

🎿 Outdoor & Activities

- **Ski Storage:** Secure storage for skis and snowboarding gear
- **Hiking Trails:** Direct access to some of Norway’s best hiking routes
- **Ice Climbing:** Located near famous ice climbing routes
- **Terrace with Views:** Enjoy stunning sunrise and sunset views from the lodge
- **BBQ & Fire Pit:** Outdoor cooking and a cozy evening around the fire

🚗 Parking & Accessibility

- **Private Parking:** Free, secure parking on-site
- **Public Transport:** Bus connections from Oslo and nearby cities
- **Shuttle Service:** Available upon request for an extra fee

About Adventure Base

Adventure Base is designed for explorers and thrill-seekers who want to experience the best of Rjukan’s outdoor activities. Whether you're a climber, hiker, or winter sports enthusiast, this lodge is your perfect retreat.

🏡 Accommodation & Layout

- **Capacity:** Up to **10 guests**
- **Total Size:** 100 m²
- **Bedrooms:** 3 comfortable rooms (2x 4-person bunk beds, 1x 2-person room)
- **Living Room:** Open space with large windows and beautiful views
- **Heating:** Electric heating and wood-burning fireplace

🍽️ Kitchen & Dining

- Fully stocked **kitchen**
- **Appliances:** Fridge, oven, stove, microwave, coffee maker, dishwasher
- **Dining Table:** Seats up to 10 people
- **Outdoor Dining:** Enjoy meals on the private terrace

🛁 Bathroom & Wellness

- **Bathrooms:** 2 modern bathrooms with showers
- **Private Sauna:** Unwind after an adventure-filled day
- **Hot Tub (Available on request):** Relax under the stars

🎿 Outdoor & Activities

- **Climbing & Skiing:** Just minutes away from world-famous climbing and ski routes
- **Hiking & Nature Walks:** Trails start right outside the lodge
- **BBQ Area:** Outdoor cooking with a grill
- **Fireplace:** Cozy evenings inside with a wood-burning stove

🚗 Parking & Accessibility

- **Private Parking:** Free, secure parking on-site
- **Public Transport:** Nearby bus stops for easy access
- **Airport Transfers:** Available upon request
