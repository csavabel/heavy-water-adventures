Discover Rjukan: The Adventure Capital

An outdoor paradise in the heart of Norway

Rjukan is a breathtaking town in Norway, famous for its stunning landscapes and extreme sports opportunities. Whether you're an experienced adventurer or a first-time explorer, Rjukan has something for everyone.

From world-class ice climbing to scenic hiking trails and thrilling bungee jumps, every visit is a new experience.

More About Rjukan

Rjukan is a UNESCO-listed town with a deep historical and cultural significance. It is home to the famous Vemork Power Station, the site of the WWII heavy water sabotage mission.

For history lovers, the Vemork Museum is a must-visit.

Plan your visit and enjoy everything this adventure paradise has to offer!
