1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve your browsing experience by remembering preferences and providing anonymized tracking data.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use different types of cookies to enhance your experience on our website:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly. These cookies cannot be disabled.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: Help us analyze how visitors use our website to improve user experience.
  • Functional Cookies: Enable personalized features such as remembering language preferences.
  • Marketing/Advertising Cookies: Used for targeted advertising and tracking conversions.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our website, such as:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks website traffic and user behavior.
  • Facebook Pixel: Helps measure the effectiveness of our advertisements.
  • Embedded Content: Some pages may include embedded content from external platforms (e.g., YouTube, Google Maps) that set their own cookies.
